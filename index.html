About Us

NOVA-ECO PACKAGING (U) LTD is a Ugandan private company conceived in 2019 and incorporated in 2024 to innovate affordable and environmentally friendly packaging solutions, especially for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs). Since then, we have evolved from a small informal entity into a formally established business.

Our dedicated production facility enables us to innovate better products, serve our customers more effectively, and further our vision of transforming the packaging industry. We prioritize innovation and sustainability, empowering businesses and individuals to make a positive and lasting impact on the environment.

We are striving to become the leading provider of eco-packaging solutions in the region, fostering a culture of innovation and sustainability, and inspiring a national movement towards a waste-free future.

Steering Africa towards a plastic-free future.
